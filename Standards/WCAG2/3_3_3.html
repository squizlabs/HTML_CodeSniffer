Success Criterion 3.3.3: Error Suggestion (Level AA)

If an input error is automatically detected and suggestions for correction are known, then the suggestions are provided to the user, unless it would jeopardize the security or purpose of the content.

(From Understanding WCAG 2.0: Understanding Success Criterion 3.3.3)

Discussion

What is sufficient?

The intent of this Success Criterion is to not only identify the errors, but also inform the user of how to correct it, possibly including suggestions where they make sense - as sometimes it is not immediately obvious. It is aimed mainly at users with learning difficulties, who may require several attempts before entering a correct value.

The examples in the Understanding document suggest that you don't need to necessarily provide an exhaustive list of the possible values, rather that additional help is at least provided.
