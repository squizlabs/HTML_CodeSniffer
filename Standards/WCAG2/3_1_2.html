Success Criterion 3.1.2: Language of Parts (Level AA)

The human language of each passage or phrase in the content can be programmatically determined except for proper names, technical terms, words of indeterminate language, and words or phrases that have become part of the vernacular of the immediately surrounding text.

(From Understanding WCAG 2.0: Understanding Success Criterion 3.1.2)

Discussion

Summary

This is an extension of Success Criterion 3.1.1, where the author marks all tags where a change in language is involved, not just the primary language. All tags accept the lang (and/or xml:lang) attributes.

Details of acceptable language tags, and which of lang and/or xml:lang is acceptable, is described in Success Criterion 3.1.1.
