Success Criterion 2.3.2: Three Flashes (Level AAA)

Web pages do not contain anything that flashes more than three times in any one second period.

(From Understanding WCAG 2.0: Understanding Success Criterion 2.3.2)

Discussion

Summary

This is a stricter version of Success Criterion 2.3.1, which removes the exception for flashing (more than 3 times per second) where the flashing area is small enough.

Sniff Coverage

Note that "[Standard]" will be replaced with "WCAG2A", "WCAG2AA" or "WCAG2AAA", depending on the level being tested.

Notices
