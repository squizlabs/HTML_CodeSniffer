Success Criterion 2.4.6: Headings and Labels (Level AA)

Headings and labels describe topic or purpose.

(From Understanding WCAG 2.0: Understanding Success Criterion 2.4.6)

Discussion

Summary

The purpose of this Success Criterion is fairly self-explanatory. With appropriately descriptive headings and labels, users are able to better grasp the structure and organisation of the content contained within.

Labels and headings do not need to be lengthy [to be descriptive]. A word, or even a single character, may suffice if it provides an appropriate cue to finding and navigating content.
